About Kampala skills connect

Founded in 2024, Kampala skills connect was created to bridge the gap between quality local artisans and the community that needs them. We understand the challenge of finding reliable, verified, and professional service providers in a fast-growing city like Kampala.

Our platform verifies identities, screens experience, and collects honest feedback, ensuring that every time you hire a professional through our link, you receive excellent value and trustworthy service. We are committed to supporting local Skills and enhancing the service economy of Uganda.

Our Core Values

  • Trust & Verification: Every provider is vetted.
  • Fairness: Transparent pricing and fair wages for local experts.
  • Efficiency: Quick connections and fast service delivery.
